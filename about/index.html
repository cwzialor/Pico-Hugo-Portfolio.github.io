Hi, here's a bit about me

I’m Caspar, a student studying an M.A. in New Media Studies at Shanghai Jiao Tong University graduating in March 2026. My research is conducted at the intersection between User Experience (UX), Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Netnography.

Before my research degree, I worked as a Video Project Manager at Innovative Language Learning in Japan.

When I’m not researching or writing - you can find me making videos, producing music or tinkering in Linux.
